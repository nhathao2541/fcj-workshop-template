During my participation in the **First Cloud AI Journey (FCAJ)** program, I had the opportunity to learn about Cloud technology, especially services on the AWS platform. The program helped me gain more practical knowledge and better understand how to work in a technology environment.

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment in the program was friendly and comfortable. Members were always willing to support me when I faced difficulties during the learning and practice process. This helped me become more confident in sharing ideas and learning new knowledge.

I think the program could organize more activities for members to interact, connect, and share experiences with each other.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
The mentor guided and supported me during my learning process about AWS and helped me solve problems during practice. The mentor explained clearly and helped me understand how to search for information and solve problems by myself instead of only providing answers.

The Team Admin also supported me well by providing learning materials and necessary information to help me complete the program.

**3. Relevance between Work and Major**  
The content of the program was suitable for my major, especially knowledge related to systems, networks, and information technology. Through FCAJ, I had the opportunity to apply my knowledge into practice and learn more about Cloud Computing.

**4. Learning Opportunities & Skill Development**  
During the program, I learned how to use AWS Console, AWS CLI, and explored how to deploy services on the Cloud. In addition, I improved my teamwork skills, information searching skills, and problem-solving skills.

**5. Culture & Teamwork Spirit**  
I experienced the support and knowledge sharing among team members during the program. Everyone was willing to help each other when facing difficulties, creating a positive and friendly learning environment.

**6. Policies / Benefits for Interns**  
The FCAJ program provided good support with learning materials, practice environments, and mentor guidance. This helped me easily approach AWS, learn new knowledge, and improve my skills during the internship.

### Other Questions

- What are you **most satisfied with** during the internship?  
I was most satisfied with the opportunity to experience real Cloud technology and learn from mentors and team members. The program helped me better understand AWS and gave me direction for developing my career in the Cloud field.

- What do you think the company **should improve** for future interns?  
In my opinion, the program could provide more practical sessions and experience-sharing activities from experienced members to help interns better understand future work.

- If you introduce this program to your friends, would you **recommend them to join this internship**? Why?  
I would recommend the FCAJ program to friends who are interested in Cloud Computing because it is a good opportunity to learn new knowledge, practice AWS, and develop necessary skills for future careers.

### Suggestions & Expectations

I hope the FCAJ program will continue to develop and provide more practical exercises so that students can apply their knowledge to real projects.

I would like to thank the program for creating opportunities for me to learn, gain experience, and develop myself in the field of Cloud Computing.
